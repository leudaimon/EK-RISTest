LV 'LV1.5 Heavy - Luna'

Family: A-4

Our heaviest rocket thus far, this LV has a total of 15 engines considering all stages. This makes it rather unreliable, but allows us to make it into the moon. I sports 4 stages (and a half), all using our new thrid generation tanks. The first stage is based on RD-103 engines, with 2 engines in the core sustainer and 2 boosters. Upper stages have clusters of 7 and 3 AJ10-27s, and the final upper stage for TLI has a single XASR-1.

Stages

  1. RD103 Heavy Tank III
  2. AJ10-27 Hepta
  3. AJ10-27 Tri
  4. TLI unguided stage
