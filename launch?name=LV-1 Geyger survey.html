Launch 'LV-1 Geyger survey'

Date: 1951-12-28

Vehicle: LV-1 Orbital Vehicle

Payload: Sat0

Destination: Earth Atmosphere

Result: Stage 1 Failure

After developing a geiger counter capable of operating in space, we tried to send it into a polar orbit to attain information of the radiation environment around the globe. Unfortunately the first stage engine failed early in the flight.
