Payload 'Hope Cockpit'

Mission to Reach Space

Launched by Hope III

Date: 1951-04-25

Destination: Sub-orbital

Result: Success

In one of the most daring missions ever attempted, these two brave ASTRONAUTS set out on a journey to heavens. By cresting to an apoapsis of 164km, they have become the first from the MSA to reach space and have now returned home safely.
