Launch 'Lightning-I Alpha'

Date: 1951-01-11

Vehicle: Lightning-I

Payload: None

Destination: Sub-orbital

Result: Success

Completely successful inaugural flight of the Lightning-I Sounding Rocket. Reached apogee of 101km and successfully completed the Karman Line and Sounding Rocket (Easy) Contracts!
