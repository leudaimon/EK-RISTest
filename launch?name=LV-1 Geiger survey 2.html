Launch 'LV-1 Geiger survey 2'

Date: 1952-01-10

Vehicle: LV-1 Orbital Vehicle

Payload: Sat0

Destination: Earth polar orbit

Result: Success

In the second try, we managed to put a geiger counter in a polar orbit around Earth. The data obtained by this experiment proved invaluable to our understanding of our magnetic field and the harshness of space.
